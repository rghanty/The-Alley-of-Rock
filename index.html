#5

Led Zeppelin

Formed in 1968, the British rock band Led Zeppelin rose to fame in the early 1970s after their eponymously titled debut studio album Led Zeppelin. Consisting of singer Robert Plant (right), lead guitarist Jimmy Page (center), bassist John Paul Jones (left) and drummer John Bonham (top), this band revolutionised hard rock and blues with their energetic performances and mesmerizing discography.

#4

Metallica

Hailed as one of the greatest heavy metal bands of all time with their fast tempos, and aggressive musicianship, Metallica has been part of the heavy metal scene since the 1980s. Gaining commercial success with their third album Master Of Puppets, which is cited as the band's best work, Metallica made sure they were there to stay.
